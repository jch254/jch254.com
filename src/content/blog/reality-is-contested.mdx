## The line between truth and fabrication

The bigger shift is scarier and more profound: as generative AI improves, the line between truth and fabrication dissolves. A real video of a shooting and an AI-generated video of a shooting are becoming indistinguishable. What counts as real anymore?

Even the chorus of G9ngstab9by's AI freestyle captures this new tension and awareness of AI being a defence for criminal offences, shootings and murders:

> "Put three in that bitch and he ain't die, damn
> That wasn't me, that was AI."

The future isn't just about better tools. It's about living in a world where reality itself is contested.
