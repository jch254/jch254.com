Joey Bada$$'s Dark Aura hits different when you've been through the chaos of tech. The layoffs, the uncertainty, the late-night coding sprints with no safety net. That shadow can feel heavy - and sometimes darker than just career moves.

But the message underneath? Survive. Adapt. Keep moving. Whether you're rolling through layoffs or wrestling with your own mind, resilience isn't about pretending it's easy — it's about refusing to quit.

Let's keep the conversation open. Mental health matters. Suicide prevention matters. We lift each other by refusing to stay silent.

Peace and respect to anyone fighting their own "dark aura." You're not alone.
