For over a decade I lived under the weight of prescriptions that added up to more than **100mg of morphine equivalents a day**: 50mg of oxycodone (as strong as heroin, stronger than morphine) plus 240mg of codeine. This wasn't a back-alley deal — this was doctors, clipboards, repeat scripts. White coats, legal pads, and a chemical leash.

## The cost

Opioids dulled pain but also ambition, sleep, and years of my life. Time blurred into doses and withdrawals, with "treatment" slowly becoming dependence.

## The turning point

In March 2025 I started weaning/tapering with help from my GP. Withdrawal was brutal — sweat, sleepless nights, nerves screaming — but clarity followed.

Onwards and upwards <3
